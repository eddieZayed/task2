Taybeh-Ramallah

Taybeh is a Christian Palestinian village in the West Bank, 15 kilometers (9.3 miles) northeast of Jerusalem[3] and 12 kilometers (7.5 miles) northeast of Ramallah, in the Ramallah and al-Bireh Governorate of Palestine. It is 850 meters (2,790 feet) above sea level. According to the Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics, Taybeh had a population of 1,340 in 2017.[1]
